About Us

Sibambiseni Group is a Non-Profit-Organisation(NPO)Which was established in 2006 as a support Group, And it has grown to a point that we now have a social worker on site who deals with social issues

We also have a safe park where kids play.We have 10 Child Youth Care workers(CYC) who assist kids with their homeworks after school, they also do various household visits in terms of identifying challenges within those homes and report back to relevent stake holders such as Derpartment of Social Developement (DSD) and home affairs for child`s birth certficates if need be and and also assist with application of social grants for surviving kids after parent(s) have passed away

We have various campaignes or activities like drug and substance abuse, we also have a program called YOLO (you only live once) where by we visit local schools in extending awareness on issues like social ills, drugs and other substance abuse, carreer guidance, child pregnancy and woman and child abuse.

Communitty programs whereby we identify child headed households and elderly people then request food parcel donations for them. We also have soup kitchen for 20 children everyday who go to school on empty stomachs.
